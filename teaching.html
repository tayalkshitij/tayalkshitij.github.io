Resources for Exploring Academic Careers with a Focus on Teaching

For those passionate about education and seeking a rewarding academic career, numerous opportunities exist to focus on teaching over research. Here I provide some resources to help navigate the job market, identify teaching-focused positions, and gain insights into the various aspects of a teaching-centered academic career.

  • Teaching Tenure Track This article explores the growing popularity of "teaching professor" positions, which prioritize teaching over research, at large universities.
  • Blog Post by Geoffrey Challen This article offers guidance to candidates seeking teaching faculty roles at research universities, focusing on computer science.
  • Faculty Positions at Undergrad Institutions This article highlights the advantages of academic careers at PUIs, emphasizing their balance between teaching and research.
  • CS Education Research : Scope, Questions, and Careers Education research focuses on understanding and improving the teaching and learning of computing, while addressing questions about pedagogy, access, and equity in the discipline. This article serves as a very good introductory resource to understand the scope of Education Research, outlining its scope, key questions, relevant conferences, and pathways for pursuing it as a career choice.
