I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science & Engineering at University of Minnesota, Twin Cities. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Vladimir Cherkassky and Prof. Ju Sun. Simultaneously, I am pursuing the minor degree (doctoral) in Health Informatics. Before starting my Ph.D. study, I have obtained my Master and Bachelor degree in Computer Science at Jilin University where I was advised by Prof. Liang Hu.

I am interested in machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, image processing, and the general applications of machine learning/deep learning in healthcare and medical settings.
