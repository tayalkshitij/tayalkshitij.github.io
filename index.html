Kshitij Tayal (he/him)

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Minnesota - Twin Cities, working under the guidance of Prof. Vipin Kumar. My research is centered on pushing the boundaries of data-driven approaches, commonly known as machine learning, to solve scientific problems. I focus mostly on cross-disciplinary work, where I employ capabilities of machine learning to navigate the complexities of generalization with noisy, heterogenous and incomplete training data. I am currently on the job market for positions that start in Summer/Fall 2023.

Prior to joining the Ph.D. program, I worked at Tata Research Labs , India for two years. I have also spent nearly a year at Amazon , where I focused on applied machine learning research, including natural language processing and graph neural networks.

I always welcome the opportunity to collaborate and discuss my research. Please feel free to reach out to me via email if you're interested
